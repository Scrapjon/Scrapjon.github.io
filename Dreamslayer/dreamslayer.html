Oliver Moloney: Dreamslayer

Dreamslayer Steam Page

During my time in the Game Art, Design and Programming course at Swinburne, I worked on the game Dreamslayer as a year-long project.
In this project, we were to use Unreal Engine 5 My main contributions to the project were:

  • Developing, testing and debugging enemy AI and behaviours based around the framework provided by Evelyn Schwab.
  • Development of a system which allows enemies to jump between platforms on different elevations.
  • Development and modification of query systems for enemy AI
  • Developing, testing and debugging of ability systems to be used by enemy characters.
  • Implimentation of every sound within the game using Unreal Engine's MetaSounds.
  • Sound design for abilities.
  • Composing the game's soundtrack.
  • Leading and managing a team during the design process of the enemies.
