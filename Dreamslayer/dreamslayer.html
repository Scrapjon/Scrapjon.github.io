Oliver Moloney: Dreamslayer

Dreamslayer Steam Page

During my time in the Game Art, Design, and Programming course at Swinburne, I worked on the game Dreamslayer as a year-long project using Unreal Engine 5. My main contributions to the project were:

  • Developing, testing, and debugging enemy AI and behaviors based on the framework provided by Evelyn Schwab.
  • Development of a system allowing enemies to jump between platforms on different elevations.
  • Development and modification of query systems for enemy AI.
  • Developing, testing, and debugging ability systems for enemy characters.
  • Implementation of all in-game sounds using Unreal Engine's MetaSounds.
  • Sound design for abilities.
  • Composing the game's soundtrack.
  • Leading and managing a team during the design process of the enemies.

The credits given to me were:

  • Lead AI Developer
  • Sound Designer
  • Soundtrack Composer / "Music Dude"
